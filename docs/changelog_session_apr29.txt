STATUS EFFECT RULES (new)
  - Stacks = total damage charges. 1 stack = 1 damage trigger.
  - Burn   : deals 1 damage every second, loses 1 stack per second.
  - Poison : deals 1 damage before each enemy attack, loses 1 stack per attack.
  - Freeze : each stack = 1 second of freeze (additive).
