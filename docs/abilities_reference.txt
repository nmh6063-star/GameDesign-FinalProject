--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STATUS EFFECT RULES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  POISON
    - Triggers before each enemy attack.
    - Deals damage equal to current stack count, then reduces stacks by 1.
    - Example: 15 stacks → deals 15 before first attack, 14 before second, etc.
    - Total damage (15 stacks): 15+14+13+...+1 = 120

  BURN
    - Triggers every real second.
    - Deals damage equal to current stack count, then reduces stacks by 1.
    - Example: 3 stacks → deals 3 at second 1, 2 at second 2, 1 at second 3.
    - Total damage (3 stacks): 3+2+1 = 6

  FREEZE
    - Each stack = 1 second of frozen timer (additive, stacks are cumulative).
    - Completely pauses the enemy's action/attack timer while active.
    - No damage component.

  CHARM
    - Enemy's next attack is redirected at another enemy.
    - Consumes 1 stack per attack resolved.

  TIME STOP  (special status — distinct from Freeze)
    - Applied only by the Time Stop ability (rank 7).
    - Duration: 10 seconds real time (stored as time_stop_until_ms).
    - Blocks the enemy's attack timer while active (same effect as Freeze but
      tracked separately — a frozen and time-stopped enemy respects both timers).
    - Enemies under Time Stop take 50% more damage from all direct hits.
    - Indicator: ⏱ Stop Xs shown on affected enemies, counting down each second.
    - No damage component.

  SHIELD
    - Absorbs incoming damage before HP is reduced.
    - Multiple shields stack additively.

  NOTE: Burn and Poison DOT damage does NOT benefit from attack_bonus,
  clone multipliers, or poison apple charges. Only direct damage does.

RANK 1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Strike (DEFAULT)
    Deal 8 × rank damage to current enemy.  (R1: 8, R2: 16, R3: 24 …)

  Mend
    Heal 5% of missing HP.
    Then deal 10% of the amount healed as direct damage to the current enemy.
    Example: 100 HP missing → heal 5, deal 1 damage to enemy.

  Venom
    Apply Poison (8 stacks) to current enemy.

  Ember
    Apply Burn (3 stacks) to ALL enemies.

  Guard
    Gain 5 Shield.

  Critical
    50% chance: deal 5 damage to ALL enemies.
    Otherwise: deal 5 damage to current enemy.

  Refresh
    Gain +1 mana pipe.

  --- Dark ---

  Poison Apple  [triggers on shot, rank 1]
    Grant 3 poison apple charges.
    While charges remain, each shot deals +10% bonus damage
    but costs 2 self-HP per shot. Charges are consumed one at a time.
    (UI note: next 3 mana-ball slots should appear black — not yet implemented.)

  --- Gambler ---

  Buy In  [triggers on merge, rank 1]
    75% chance: heal 10 HP.
    25% chance: take 10 damage.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
RANK 3
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Power Slash (DEFAULT)
    Deal 25 damage to current enemy.

  Pollution
    Double the current poison stack count on the current enemy.

  Fireball
    Hit 2 random enemies for 8 damage each.
    Each hit also applies Burn (8 stacks) to that enemy.

  Ice Shield
    Gain 10 Shield.
    Freeze current enemy for 5 seconds.

  Reinforce
    Gain +2 attack damage for this battle.
    This bonus applies to all direct damage only (not DOT like burn/poison).
    Stacks with multiple uses.

  Convert
    Upgrade 1 random ball in the box by +1 rank (capped at rank 7).

  Echo Shot
    Reapply the most recently resolved damage + effect.
    Cannot echo another echo shot.

  Charm
    Apply 1 charm stack to ALL enemies.
    Each enemy redirects their next attack at a random other enemy.

  Thunder Fang
    Deal 5% of current HP to the active target and 5% to every other enemy.
    Apply 5 ⚡ thunder stacks to the target and 3 to all others.
    Thunder stacks persist forever and cause chain damage (see Thunder Debuff below).

  Decay
    Permanently reduce the active enemy's max HP by 10 (5 stacks × 2) for this battle.
    Their current HP is immediately clamped down to the new max.
    Effect stacks across multiple uses; can never reduce max HP below 1.

  Regeneration
    Heal the player for 3 HP at the end of each of the next 10 seconds.
    Total: 30 HP over 10 s. Multiple Regenerations run independently.

  --- Dark ---

  Slow Down  [rank 3]
    NOT IMPLEMENTED.

RANK 4
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Cleave (DEFAULT)
    Deal 15 damage to ALL enemies.

  Greater Heal
    Heal 30% of missing HP.

  Bomb Orb
    After 10 seconds (real time), deal 50 damage to ALL enemies.
    A live countdown (💣 Bomb Xs) is shown on all enemies while the fuse is active.

  Chain Spark
    Hit up to 3 different enemies sequentially.
    First hit: 10 damage. Second hit: 20 damage. Third hit: 40 damage.

  Mirror Shield
    Reflect the next 2 incoming damage instances back to the attacker.
    Does not reflect status effects.

  Corrupt Field
    Apply Poison (12 stacks) to ALL enemies.
    For this shoot only: all enemies that have poison stacks deal 20% less damage
    to the player. (⚗ Wkn 1 indicator shown while active.)

  Tide Turner
    When this ball is shot alongside X other balls in the same turn:
    after all on_shot effects resolve, deal X × (total damage dealt by those balls)
    as bonus direct damage to the active enemy.
    Example: shot with 2 others that dealt 30 damage total → deal 3 × 30 = 90 bonus.

  Weakness Brand
    Mark the active enemy for 3 shoots: they take 30% more direct damage.
    (🔻 Brand ×N indicator on enemy status bar.)
    Refreshes to 3 if applied again while still active.

  Lifesteal Field
    For the rest of this battle: heal 10% of every direct damage instance dealt
    to any enemy. Separate from Siphon (which heals from DoT only).
    (🩸 Lifesteal shown in player status bar.)

  Fortress
    Gain 50 Shield immediately.
    Take 15 raw HP damage (bypasses shield, Gatekeeper, and Fragile; HP floored at 1).

  --- Dark ---

  Care Drop  [triggers on merge, rank 4]
    Spawn 5 rank 1–2 normal balls from the top of the box.

RANK 5
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Critical Edge (DEFAULT)
    Deal 25 or 30 damage to one enemy (chosen randomly).

  Freeze Wave
    Freeze ALL enemies for 3 seconds (3 stacks).

  Giant Orb
    One random ball in the box gains:
      - ×3 attack damage multiplier
      - ×2 size (radius doubles; collision and visuals both update)
    Does not trigger twice. Cannot be applied to a ball already giant.

  Consume Core
    Remove 1 random ball from the box (permanently for this battle).
    Then deal 100 damage to current enemy.

  Upgrade Pulse
    Upgrade 1 random ball in the box by +1 rank (capped at rank 7).

  Poison Rain
    Activate a 3-shoot Rain mode (☣ Rain ×N shown on enemies):
    • Enemies no longer lose poison stacks — instead stacks INCREASE by 1 each tick.
    • Every direct hit on an enemy adds +2 poison stacks to them.
    The effect expires after 3 ball drops.

  Time Drift
    Slow game time to 40% speed for 10 seconds.
    For the first 5 seconds: all damage you would take is stored instead.
    For the final 5 seconds: stored damage is dealt back to all enemies
    as continuous DOT (divided into 5 ticks, 1 per second).

  Contagion
    Copy poison, burn, and charm stacks plus remaining freeze duration from the
    current enemy onto one random other enemy (adds to their existing debuffs).

  Chaos Slash
    Strike 5 random targets for 15 direct damage each.
    Targets are chosen from ALL living enemies PLUS the player (equal weight).
    Each time the player is hit: +1 Fragile stack.
    Fragile: +20% damage taken per stack (all stacks clear at the end of this shoot).

  Guillotine
    Deal 25% of the active enemy's MISSING HP as direct damage.
    Example: enemy has 200 max HP and 80 current HP → missing 120 → deal 30.
    Deals at least 1 damage even if the enemy is at full health.

  --- Dark ---

  Biggering  [rank 5]
    Sets merge_growth to 8. INCOMPLETE — damage multiplier not yet wired.

RANK 6
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Meteor Crash (DEFAULT)
    Deal 30 damage to ALL enemies.

  Full Recovery
    Restore HP equal to 30% of max HP.

  Chaos Rain
    If you have 1+ mana: spend 1 mana and spawn 6 random balls (rank 1–3).
    Otherwise: spawn 3 random balls (rank 1–3) for free.

  Overcharge
    Gain +5 attack damage for this battle.
    Applies to all direct damage (stacks with Reinforce).

  Mass Morph
    Upgrade ALL rank 1 and rank 2 balls currently in the box by +1 rank.

  Reflect Wall
    Reflect ALL incoming damage for 12 seconds.

  Giant Core
    One random rank 1–5 ball in the box gains:
      - ×3 attack damage multiplier
      - Triggers twice per shot
      - ×2 size (radius doubles)
    Cannot be applied to a ball already giant.

  Siphon
    For the rest of this battle: heal for 10% of all DOT damage dealt to enemies
    (_damage_enemy_dot paths).

  Gatekeeper
    The next 3 times the player takes damage:
    50% of each hit is converted into Shield (added immediately).
    The other 50% still hits (reduced by any existing shield after gain).
    Charges shown as 🔰 GK×N in the player status bar.

  Storm Surge
    Deal 10% of each enemy's max HP as direct damage to ALL living enemies.
    Apply 20 ⚡ thunder stacks to each enemy hit.
    See Thunder Debuff below for chain mechanics.

  Second Wind
    Passive trigger: whenever HP drops below 30% of max (crossing threshold):
      First trigger this battle → heal 40% of max HP.
      Subsequent triggers      → heal 10% of missing HP.
    One trigger per low-HP event (cooldown clears when HP recovers to ≥30%).
    (💨 2nd Wind / 💨 2nd Wind+ shown in player status bar.)

  Overkill
    Immediately deal 40 direct damage to the active enemy.
    For the rest of this battle: when any direct hit kills an enemy, the excess
    damage (amount − enemy HP before hit) carries over to the next alive enemy.

  --- Dark ---

  An Eye For An Arm  [triggers on merge, rank 6]
    Deal rank × 1.5 damage to current enemy.
    Take rank ÷ 2 damage to self.

RANK 7
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Final Judgment (DEFAULT)
    Deal 12 damage to current enemy 4 times (48 total).
    Each hit benefits from Giant Orb / attack bonuses.

  Apocalypse
    Deal 8 damage to ALL enemies 6 times (48 total per enemy).
    Each hit benefits from attack bonuses.

  Resurrection
    Set a one-time revive: if you die this battle, revive once with low HP.
    Cannot stack multiple resurrections.

  Time Stop
    Remove ALL balls currently in the box.
    All enemies enter a 10-second Time Stop state (⏱ Stop Xs indicator):
    • They cannot attack while stopped.
    • They take 50% more damage from all hits while stopped.
    (Distinct from Freeze — stacks separately.)

  Magic Flood
    Apply 10 random enchantments total, each targeting a random alive enemy:
      - Poison: 8 stacks
      - Burn: 3 stacks
      - Freeze: 3 stacks (3 seconds)

  Miracle Cascade
    Trigger one random ability from rank 3 pool,
    one random ability from rank 4 pool,
    one random ability from rank 5 pool.
    (Three abilities fire in sequence.)

  Sacrifice Nova
    Immediately lose 50% of current HP.
    After 10 seconds (real time), deal 500 damage to ALL enemies.

  Shower
    Over 10 seconds, spawn 10 balls (rank 1–3) one per second.
    Each ball drops at a random X position spread across the full box width.

  Echo
    For the rest of this battle: every DOT damage instance triggers twice.

  Baator's Flame
    Convert ALL DoT status stacks on every living enemy into Burn:
      Poison  → Burn ×1.5  (e.g. 20 poison → 30 burn stacks)
      Thunder → Burn ×2    (e.g. 10 thunder → 20 burn stacks)
      Freeze  → Burn ×10   (remaining seconds × 10 burn stacks)
    Source stacks are cleared; only Burn remains.

  Thunder Strike
    For each living enemy that carries at least 1 ⚡ thunder stack:
      Deal (enemy's current HP) × (2% per thunder stack) as direct damage.
    Example: enemy with 200 HP and 15 stacks takes 200 × 0.30 = 60 damage.

  Elbaph's Power
    For 15 seconds, all balls on the board grow continuously:
      Size multiplier:   1.0× → 2.0×   (full-size at 15 s)
      Direct damage mult: 0.5× → 1.5×  (150% at 15 s)
    Does NOT affect DoT damage.  Ball collision shapes scale in real-time.

  --- Dark ---

  Clone  [triggers on activation, rank 7]
    Spawn a copy of this ball at rank 7.
    Add 1 clone stack: each stack permanently doubles all direct damage.
    Permanently reduce max HP by 5%.
    Max 4 stacks (5 total activations).
    Example with 3 stacks: direct damage is multiplied by ×8.

  --- Gambler ---

  Roll For Initiative  [triggers on activation, rank 7]
    50% chance: all enemies execute all of their actions 3 times.
    50% chance: deal 100 damage to current enemy AND trigger one random
    rank-7 rank ability (Apocalypse / Time Stop / Magic Flood /
    Miracle Cascade / Sacrifice Nova / Shower / Echo).

THUNDER DEBUFF MECHANIC
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Applied by: Thunder Fang (R3), Storm Surge (R6).
  Displayed as: ⚡ Thdr N  on the enemy's status bar (yellow).
  Stacks last forever (never decay on their own).

  When any enemy with N thunder stacks takes D damage (direct or DOT):
    1. That enemy takes an extra D × N% damage (its own stacks resonate).
    2. Every OTHER living enemy with M thunder stacks also takes D × M% as DoT.

  Example — A(20), B(30), C(40) stacks; 100 direct damage on B:
    B → 100 + 100×30% = 130 total
    A → 100×20% = 20 DoT
    C → 100×40% = 40 DoT

  Chain damage uses the DOT path so it bypasses attack_bonus and clone stacks
  but CAN trigger the Siphon heal and dot_echo (Echo) effects.
  Recursion is guarded — thunder chain damage does NOT re-chain.
